# KPI Definitions

This document explains the KPIs used in the Amazon Fulfillment Analytics dashboard.

---

## 1️⃣ On-Time Delivery Percentage
**Definition:**  
Percentage of orders delivered within the acceptable delivery window.

**Business Value:**  
Measures fulfillment reliability and customer satisfaction.

---

## 2️⃣ Orders by Region
**Definition:**  
Total number of orders handled by each region.

**Business Value:**  
Identifies high-demand regions and workload distribution.

---

## 3️⃣ Order Status Distribution
**Definition:**  
Breakdown of orders by status (Delivered, Processing, Delayed).

**Business Value:**  
Highlights operational health and pending workload.

---

## 4️⃣ Daily Cost Trend
**Definition:**  
Total fulfillment cost aggregated by day.

**Business Value:**  
Detects cost spikes and daily inefficiencies.

---

## 5️⃣ Average Delivery Time by Region
**Definition:**  
Average delivery duration for each region.

**Business Value:**  
Helps optimize regional logistics performance.

---

## 6️⃣ Delay Reason Analysis
**Definition:**  
Count of delayed shipments by delay reason.

**Business Value:**  
Identifies root causes of delays.

---

## 7️⃣ Average Cost per Warehouse
**Definition:**  
Average fulfillment cost incurred by each warehouse.

**Business Value:**  
Evaluates warehouse-level cost efficiency.

---

## 8️⃣ Average Cost per Order
**Definition:**  
Overall average cost to fulfill one order.

**Business Value:**  
High-level cost efficiency indicator for management.
